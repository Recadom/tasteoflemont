About

A Culinary Journey

Taste of Lemont is a fun, family-friendly event - made possible in part by Visionary Sponsor Citgo - which will feature some of the best food and beverages that the Lemont community can offer, as well a concert from one of Chicagoland’s top bands - Mike and Joe!

Band

Mike and Joe

Mike and Joe is one of the nation’s top rock cover bands. Its sets typically feature hits from artists ranging from U2 and Maroon 5, to Justin Timberlake and the Dave Matthews Band. While its shows most often feature hits from the 90s through today, Mike and Joe throws in songs from classic rock royalty such as The Eagles and Fleetwood Mac as well. The LHSEF is excited to welcome Mike and Joe’s stable of loyal fans to the Taste of Lemont in 2017!

- Culinary Chef - Sartre
